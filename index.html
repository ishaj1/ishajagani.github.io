The world today has come to a standstill thanks to the deadly Covid19 virus. Millions have been infected and many thousands have died.

As we scramble today to find a cure and a potential vaccine to help save lives and bring the world back to normal, one of the treatment options that looks promising is that of convalescent plasma therapy.

Recovered patients of covid19 have antibodies in their blood which could potentially help save another seriously ill patient's life.

Like many countries over the world, India has also started clinical trials to determine the efficacy of this treatment.

The Indian Council of Medical Research has currently approved 21 institutions all over the country to carry out randomized controlled clinical trials for convalescent plasma therapy.

With the total number of Covid19 cases in the country crossing 50,000 and the number of recovered Covid19 patients being more than 15,000 there is a huge potential to recruit eligible donors to donate their plasma.
