As a person who has recovered from Covid, your body has developed antibodies against the virus. These antibodies are present in the plasma component of your blood.

The antibodies from your blood can be used as a treatment option to neutralize the virus in critically ill patients and potentially help save a person's life. They may help lessen the severity of the symptoms or shorten the duration of symptoms.

The process of donating plasma is similar to blood donation but takes a little longer. The blood goes through the plasmapherisis machine and its components are separated. The red blood cells and platelets are returned into your body and the separated plasma is used for transfusion in an ill patient.

Be a hero today and donate your plasma to help save someone's life !
