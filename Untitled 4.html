PlasmaX has been created with the vision of mobilizing and creating a repository of recovered covid19 patients who are willing to donate their plasma to help potentially save lives of others who are seriously ill with the disease.

Anticipating a large number of people who will be infected by covid19 and eventually recover from the disease this website will help shorten the time taken by the concerned authorities/ hospitals to mobilize volunteers for plasma donation and in turn be swift to actually help the patients in need.

Currently this will either be possible by being a part of a clinical trial or allowing the use of the plasma to the patient on a compassionate basis.
